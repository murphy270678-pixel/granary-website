Help and support

Common Skye Companion questions

Quick answers for using the app before and during your Isle of Skye trip.

I paid but the app still shows locked content

Open the Unlock tab and tap “Restore purchase”. This reconnects your existing purchase without charging you again. If it still does not unlock, email us below.

Do I need an account?

No. Skye Companion does not use accounts, logins or passwords. Everything is stored locally on your device.

The weather or map is not loading

Weather updates and map tiles need a live connection. Mobile signal can be limited in parts of Skye, but information you have saved remains available offline.

I found something inaccurate in the guide

Please tell us. Opening hours, prices and closures can change. Include the name of the place and what has changed so we can correct it.
